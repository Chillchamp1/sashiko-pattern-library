⬡ Fibonacci Snowflake

Fibonacci Snowflake order 2. Phase sequence = 4th binary Fibonacci string "01001010" mirrored → 16-element palindrome with 4-fold symmetry. (Monnerot-Dumaine, 2009)

By assigning colours to the lines you change how the animation creates the stitching order. The aim is to find a combination that is easy and fun to stitch. Just play around with the colors and see how the animation reacts. As soon as you have found the right combination it will be quite obvious because a complicated geometry will be broken down into easy to follow lines or curves.
